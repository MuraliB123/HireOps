Why Credit Score !

We identified key parameters such as

  • Employee's expertise
  • Hours spent on task
  • Critical need at the moment
  • Manager's performance index
To determine the availability of employees on taking up newer tasks. Our suggestion helps to reduce over-workload as well as under-workload thereby yielding better retention and growth.

Credit Score Interpretation

  • Score >= 10
    • Employee is readily available for newer tasks
  • Score 7-9
    • Employee can be considered for newer tasks
  • Score <= 6
    • Employee is not available for newer tasks
